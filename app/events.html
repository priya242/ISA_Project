ISA Events

Ganesh Puja

ISA's very first event is Ganesh Pooja. What's better than starting the term with blessings of BAPPA! Ganesh Chaturthi is celebrated by everyone in India and abroad with a lot of enthusiasm. It is a ten-day festival where devotees worship Lord Ganesha all over the country. He is worshiped as the god of wisdom, fortune and prosperity.

Fall Semester

Freshers Party

A casual event organised by ISA for freshers' students. This is a nice opportunity for freshers to meet their seniors and make new friends in PSU. The event is organized in Game Room at SMSU. Join us to play games like snooker, bowling, Video Games and Foosball. Delight yourself with free popcorn, drinks and snacks along with your favorite pizza!

Fall Semester

Diwali

Amid the seasonal Fall and busy term, colors and lights illuminate our homes and heart. Diwali is all about celebrations, dance and frolic, food and feast - Festival of lights! Each year, Indian Students Association, cordially invites everyone to come visit and experience the traditional Indian hues, dance forms, delicious food and a cultural evening in our very own PSU campus.

Fall Semester

Holi

Holi, the festival of colors, is one of the major festivals of India and is observed in the month of March. A day before Holi, a large bonfire is lit and is believed to burn all the evil spirits. We, at Indian Student Association, have an exciting event for you so that you can have a great spring break and a colorful start to the New Term. Come enjoy the day with us! Tasty Indian food followed by great dance and music by DJ!

Spring Semester

Sanskriti

The final event of the year!! Celebrating Indian culture in all possible ways, Sanskriti is one of PSU's grandest events. And when we say all possible ways, we mean it! From mesmerizing dance, song, band, mimicry performances to our ever stunning Indian fashion show, we have it all and much more! Not to forget, we also have delicious Indian food to satisfy your taste buds and an end to the night on a high with live DJ!
